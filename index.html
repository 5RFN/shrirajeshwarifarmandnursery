Shri Rajeshwari Farm & Nursery

100% ORGANIC / SUSTAINABLY GROWN / NHB-CERTIFIED

Over 30 Years of Organic Fruit Cultivation

Introducing Rajeshwari Farm & Nursery, nestled in the heart of Moti Mau - Kutch, where a legacy of excellence in organic fruit cultivation spans over four to three decades. With the esteemed certification from the NHB (National Horticulture Board), we proudly uphold our tradition of delivering top-tier organic produce.

At our sprawling farm, covering approximately 220 acres in Moti Mau, Kutch, we have nurtured a flourishing orchard boasting around 11,000 majestic Kesar Mango trees. Specializing in the cultivation of Organic Kesar Mangoes, alongside Watermelons, Muskmelons, Dragonfruits, Papayas, and Bananas, we ensure that every fruit bears the hallmark of unparalleled taste and purity, free from any harmful chemicals.

But our commitment doesn't stop there. We've also established a premier Nursery, dedicated to the propagation of Mango, Chiku, and a variety of other fruit plants, nurturing the future of sustainable agriculture. Trust Rajeshwari Farm & Nursery for a harvest of excellence, rooted in decades of expertise and a passion for organic farming.
